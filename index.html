About Me

I’m currently a student pursuing a Professional Science Masters in GIS at Temple. I have a wide range of skills and experience. I’ve been an organizer, I've designed and led research efforts including survey design, field work, and data collection. I have years of practice doing hands-on work in natural resource management, including wildlife surveys, wildland firefighting, and tree care. Now I'm learning about data science and the powerful tools that come with this training. I'm proficient in database design using SQL and building ETL processes. I can use python to perform non-spatial and spatial data operations, as well as javascript and leaflet for web mapping.

My Projects

Map of Squats in Berlin

This map of Berlin's most famous occupied houses (squats) is designed entirely using Open Street Map vector layers. The berlin wall has been georeferenced from a superimposed google maps image, and all stylistic choices were made from scratch.

Chloropleth Map

Chloropleth map made utilizing leaflet functionality and linking a GeoJSON file with information on Coronavirus cases per 1 million people by County in East New York State.

Proportional Symbols Map

A proportional symbolds map made utilizing leaflet functionality and linking a GeoJSON file with information on counts of Coronavirus assotiated deaths in each country in the world.

Fuel Calculator

This is a distance calculator- calculating the total distance to Philadelphia in KM from: - San Francisco - Berlin - Bangkok. It also gives the percentage of fuel remaining at the end of the trip in a 747 plane with an assumed fuel capacity of 100,000 gallons and a fuel burn rate of 12 gallons/KM.

Find the point on a world map

A fun game where a hidden location was chosen for the user to find. You must click around at the world scale until you find this location. Once you do, an icon will appear on the map permanently marking your discovery. For this I chose my home town.

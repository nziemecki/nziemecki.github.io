About Me

I’m currently a student pursuing a Professional Science Masters in GIS at Temple. I have a wide range of skills and experience. I’ve been an organizer, I've designed and led research efforts including survey design, field work, and data collection. I have years of practice doing hands-on work in natural resource management, including wildlife surveys, wildland firefighting, and tree care. Now I'm learning about data science and the powerful tools that come with this training. I'm proficient in database design using SQL and building ETL processes. I can use python to perform non-spatial and spatial data operations, as well as javascript and leaflet for web mapping.
